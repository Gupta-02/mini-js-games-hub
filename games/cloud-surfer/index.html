Welcome to Cloud Surfer!

Your Mission:

  • Ride clouds through the sky while maintaining balance
  • Collect raindrops, stars, and rainbows for points
  • Avoid thunderstorms, tornadoes, and hail storms
  • Use arrow keys to move and maintain balance
  • Press SPACE to jump to a new cloud when needed
  • Keep the balance meter in the center to stay on clouds

Balance System:

  • The balance meter shows your stability on the cloud
  • Stay centered to maintain control
  • Extreme tilting will cause you to fall off
  • Collect items to stabilize your balance

Weather Conditions:

  • Clear skies - Easy sailing with gentle winds
  • Overcast - Moderate difficulty with variable winds
  • Stormy - High difficulty with strong winds and lightning
